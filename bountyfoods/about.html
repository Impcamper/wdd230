About Us!

We are a small business specializing in organic, locally grown foods. Located in Carlsbad, California, we have many food trucks that cover the coastal area from Los Angeles to San Diego. We sell fruits and produce directly from the farmers in the area, but also some national and international suppliers. Owner-operated, we cater to walk in traffic at our storefront and our food trucks.

Our mission is to provide fresh and healthy food options made to order to our local population, and to tourists visiting the coastal areas
